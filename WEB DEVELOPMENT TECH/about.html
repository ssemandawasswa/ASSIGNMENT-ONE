About the Gala

Uganda martyrs university is a catholic university established to to educate and form learners holistically through giving them education.

The objective of this university is to see that its vision , and aim are felt among the people who go to it.

The Uganda Martyrs University Nkozi Sports Gala is an annual event that brings together students, staff, and alumni to celebrate athletic excellence and unity.

Welcome to the official page of the Uganda Martyrs University Sports Gala — a vibrant celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and university spirit!

This Sports Even is held annually at the scenic main campus of Uganda Martyrs University at Nkozi-Masaka, our Sports Gala brings together students, staff, alumni, and partners in a thrilling showcase of talent, endurance, and unity. More than just a competition, the gala is a testament to the university's commitment to holistic education — nurturing not just the mind, but also the body and spirit.

The Sports Gala features a wide array of sporting disciplines including football, netball, athletics, basketball, volleyball, and traditional games, allowing participants from all faculties and backgrounds to compete, connect, and celebrate diversity through sport.

This event is proudly organized by the university's Sports Department, in collaboration with student leaders. It stands as one of the most anticipated events on the university calendar hence promoting physical wellness, leadership, inclusion, and community engagement.

Whether you are a participant, fan, or a Sponsor, the UMU Sports Gala welcomes you to be part of this dynamic tradition that continues to grow year after year.
